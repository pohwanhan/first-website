Animes Recommendation

These are animes that I highly recommend any anime-seekers/lovers to endeavour into. There are no specific genre that I particularly go for; so the list below is a very diverse range.
Full Metal Alchemist [FMA]

  51 episodes + "Conqueror of Shambala" Movie

Adventure, Science Fantasy, Mystery, Action, Comedy
The story follows the struggles of 2 alchemist brothers who attempts human transmutation to resurrect their beloved mother; only to have their precious limbs and bodies taken away in accordance with the Law of Equivalent Exchange. This motivates the pair to pursue the truth and tries to acquire the Philosopher Stone, all in a bid to retrieve back their bodies.

9/10 ★ ★ ★ ★ ★ ★ ★ ★ ★ ☆

<Does not follow manga series, great visuals, little fillers, lots of intense cliffhangers, touching plot, comedic relieves>

Full Metal Alchemist: Brotherhood

  64 episodes
Adventure, Science Fantasy, Mystery, Action, Comedy
The characters and world are similar to Full Metal Alchemist, but the storyline and ending are different. This is fully adapted from the manga series.

8/10 ★ ★ ★ ★ ★ ★ ★ ★ ☆ ☆

< Minimal fillers, Visuals not as appealing as FMA but still good, the story is easier to follow compared to FMA>

Claymore

  64 episodes
Adventure, Dark Fantasy, Mystery, Action
Claymore warriors are half-bred Yoma and human created to eradicate Yoma who feeds on humans.

8/10 ★ ★ ★ ★ ★ ★ ★ ★ ☆ ☆

< Lots of gruesome & exciting fighting scenes, Still wishing for a 2nd season>

Another

  12 episodes
Adventure, Science Fantasy, Mystery, Action
A dark curse haunts everyone who is enrolled to Class 3-3 and there seems to only be one way to not die.

8/10 ★ ★ ★ ★ ★ ★ ★ ★ ☆ ☆

< Eerie storyline, jam packed with gore-y and gruesome freak deaths>
